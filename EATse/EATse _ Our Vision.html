    About Eatse

    We want all chefs of all backgrounds to feel comfortable to expand their cooking skills and boost their confidence in cooking for customers based on their specified meal preference. In hopes of reducing food waste and cultivating more creativity in the food industry, EATse allows customers to pioneer meals with descriptions of the ingredients in that particular meal.
